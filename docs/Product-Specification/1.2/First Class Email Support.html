First Class Email Support

Discover a Stream View Eureka Metrics

Overview

Eureka Streams is enabled to accept e-mails that will post a message to a stream or comment on a post. Users will be able to add to their contacts list the e-mail address of individual streams. Users will be able to reply to post notifications in order to comment on that particular post.

Conditions of Satisfaction

Replying to a post notification e-mail

  1. The Knowledge Worker must be able to “reply to “an e-mail received from a post in order to send an e-mail to the Eureka Streams e-mail box.

E-mail posting or commenting

  1. The Knowledge Worker must be able to post a message to a stream via e-mail.
  2. The Knowledge Worker must be able to comment on an activity in stream via e-mail.
  3. The Knowledge Worker must be able to e-mail with a signature, original e-mail text, etc. and have these extra itemsremoved allowing only the post or comment text to be processed.
  4. The Knowledge Worker must be able to send an e-mail that includes a multipart form and/or HTML and have the HTML stripped out and/or the additional multipart forms ignored allowing only the post or comment text to be processed.
  5. The Knowledge Worker must be able to e-mail a post larger than 250 characters and have the extra characters appear as a comment below their post.
  6. The Knowledge Worker must be able to e-mail a comment longer than 1,000 characters and have the extra characters appear as additional comments below their comment.

Test Plan

Replying to a post notification e-mail

  1. Verify a Knowledge Worker can “rely to” an email recieved from a post

    • Verify the ability to reply to the email
    • Verify the email was sent to the Eureka Streams e-mail box

Downloading a stream contact

  1. Verify a Knowledge Worker can add e-mail address to their contacts list on their streams

    • Verify the ability to add a contact in their contact list

E-mail posting or commenting
