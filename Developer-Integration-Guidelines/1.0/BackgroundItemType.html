BackgroundItemType

BaseObjectType BackgroundItem

Description

Enumeration for background items. In an enumeration, name() and valueOf90 are bidirectional. For our peurposes, constructor/toEnum and toString are bidirectional.

Enumeration Values

NOT_SET (“Not Initialized”) - Uninitialized value

**INTEREST (“Interest and Hobbies”) - An interest of a person

SKILL (“Skills and Specialties”) - A skill of a person

AFFILIATION (“Affiliations”) - An affiliation of a person

HONOR (“Honors and Awards”) - An honor of a person

CAPABILITY (“Capability”) - A capability for an organization

AREA_OF_STUDY (“Area of Study”) - An area of study of a person

SCHOOL_NAME (“School name”) - Name of school
